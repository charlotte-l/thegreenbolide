The Green Bolide

The Green Bolide Chassis Body Powertrain Steering Our Sponsors

The Green Bolide is a lean, mean, green machine.

With a sleek, aerodynamic carbon fibre body, a bioethanol ICE and a cutting edge electronic push-start carburetor, the Green Bolide weighs only 40kg and has an estimaged mpg of x. The Green Bolide will be appearing at Shell Eco-marathon Europe 2017 and has been created by Northumbria University's Team Nucleus.

The carbon fibre future: light, sleek and aerodynamic

Team Nucleus hand-formed the EVA foam moulds which were used in the forming of the carbon fibre body pieces. Designed to minimise drag and maximise speed and fuel efficiency, the body encompasses the wheels and car interiors to create a sleek bullet-shaped vehicle to cut through the air.

The Green Bolide's body weighs just x kg, allowing for maximum fuel efficiency. It consists of three assembled panels, with a large front window and separate engine panel.
